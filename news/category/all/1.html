Project Maverick wins again at the Central California Science Fair

3/19/2016

0 Comments

 
At the Central California Regional Science, Mathematics and Engineering Fair March 14 - 17, 2016 Project Maverick received the following awards:
- 1st place in category of Engineering (2nd year in a row)
- Sweepstakes Champion - 1st place all categories (2nd year in a row)
- 2016 Excellence in Science Award 

Project Maverick will be competing at the 2016 Intel International Science and Engineering Fair (ISEF) in Phoenix, Arizona, and California State Science Fair in Los Angeles, California.

2-Dimensional Sensors?!?! Used for Autopilot

2/25/2016

0 Comments

 
Project Maverick has gained a new pair of eyes! A new ZX gesture sensor from Sparkfun has been successfully tested and is getting ready to be implemented into Project Maverick's Autopilot System. This will allow Maverick to quickly and efficiently find obstacles and doorways before encountering them. Project Maverick and its updated Autopilot System will be featured at the Central Valley Science and Engineering Fair on March 17th from 6pm to 7pm. Please visit here for more information.
